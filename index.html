What we do

Our business specializes in resurfacing old concrete with a beautiful Limestone overlay. There is no need to tear out, our work is slip resistant, and our overlays are cooler than regular concrete. We are experts in installations in any stable surface of concrete including pool deck, back patios, front porch, sidewalk, and even on drive ways.

TEBE Limekrete proudly provides Limestone overlay services to the Houston metroplex including The Woodlands, Katy, Sugarland, Pearland, and Pasadena.
